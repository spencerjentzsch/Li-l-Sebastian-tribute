Li'l Sebastian

Hero, Mentor, Friend

Li'l Sebastian running like 5000 candles in the wind
Li'l Sebastian was Pawnee's premier mini-horse and was universally beloved. During the last Harvest Festival, he was the eighth-most-photographed thing in America, beating out the Alamo.
America's favorite miniature pony had an honorary degree from Notre Dame. His friend and lover was another mini-horse named Coconut. His favorite charity was the Afghan Institute of Learning.
Li'l Sebastian dedicated his life to battleing the following:
  • Diabetes
  • Cataracts
  • Arthritis
Please honor his memory by donating to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.
